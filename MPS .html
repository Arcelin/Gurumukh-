Multifunctional Punching & Shearing Machine (MPS)

The Multifunctional Punching & Shearing Machine (MPS) is a versatile fabrication solution designed to perform multiple sheet metal operations on a single platform, combining punching, shearing and bending functions.

Built with a heavy-duty fabricated steel frame, the MPS ensures stability and reliable performance during continuous operations. Its integrated design minimizes material handling and setup time.

These machines are widely used in fabrication workshops, electrical panel manufacturing, steel furniture production, enclosure fabrication and general job-work units.

Designed for ease of operation and long service life, the MPS from Gurumukh Machine Industries delivers dependable performance backed by strong after-sales support.
