Multipurpose Punching & Shearing Machine (MPS)

The MPS machine is a versatile fabrication solution combining punching, shearing and bending operations on a single platform, reducing setup time and improving workflow efficiency.

Built with a heavy-duty fabricated steel frame, the machine ensures stable performance and long service life even during continuous industrial use.

Widely used in fabrication workshops, electrical panel manufacturing, steel furniture production and enclosure fabrication units.

Drill Machine & Special Purpose Machine (SPM)

SPM Drill Machines are designed for high-accuracy drilling and tapping operations in repetitive and production-oriented manufacturing environments.

Ideal for automotive components, electrical panels, tool rooms and batch production workshops.
