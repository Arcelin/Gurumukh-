Treadle Shearing Machine

Manual foot-operated shearing machines designed for reliable, controlled and accurate sheet cutting without the use of electricity.

The Treadle Shearing Machine is a manual, foot-operated sheet cutting machine designed for reliable, controlled and accurate cutting without the use of electricity. Built for simplicity and durability, this machine is ideal for workshops and fabrication units that require consistent cutting performance with low operating cost.

The machine features a heavy-duty steel frame that provides stability during cutting operations, ensuring smooth movement and precise blade alignment. The treadle mechanism allows the operator to apply controlled force, resulting in clean, straight and burr-free cuts across various sheet materials.

Treadle Shearing Machines are especially suited for small to medium-scale fabrication work, where dependable performance and ease of maintenance are essential. The absence of electrical components makes the machine safe, economical and suitable for areas with limited or unreliable power supply.

Manufactured with a focus on mechanical reliability and practical usability, the Treadle Shearing Machine from Gurumukh Machine Industries delivers consistent cutting performance backed by decades of manufacturing expertise.

Size Specifications

Cutting Lengths: 2 ft, 3 ft, 4 ft, 5 ft, 6 ft, 8 ft

Material Compatibility: Mild Steel (MS), Stainless Steel (SS), Aluminium

Construction: Heavy-duty cast iron body for stable operation
