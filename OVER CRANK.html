Over Crank Power Shearing Machine

The Over Crank Power Shearing Machine is built for high cutting force, faster stroke speed and heavy-duty production requirements. The crank mechanism positioned above the cutting table provides greater mechanical advantage during each cutting cycle.

Constructed with a heavy-duty fabricated steel frame, the machine delivers reliable performance under high loads. The powerful mechanical drive system ensures smooth yet forceful cutting action even during extended production runs.

Over Crank Power Shearing Machines are widely used in heavy fabrication units, structural fabrication workshops and industrial manufacturing plants where productivity and cutting capacity are the primary concerns.
