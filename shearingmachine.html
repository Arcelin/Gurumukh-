Shearing Machines

Heavy-duty power shearing machines engineered for clean, accurate and high-productivity sheet metal cutting.

Each machine is built on a rigid welded steel frame that ensures vibration-free operation even during high-load cutting. Precision-aligned cutting mechanisms maintain uniform blade clearance for clean and burr-free cuts.

The mechanical drive system delivers stable cutting force and smooth stroke movement, enabling accurate results with minimal operator effort.

Power Shearing Machines are widely used in sheet metal fabrication, electrical panel manufacturing, HVAC ducting, steel furniture and general job-work industries.

Under Crank Power Shearing Machine

The Under Crank Power Shearing Machine is designed for balanced, stable and precision-oriented sheet cutting. The crank mechanism positioned below the table ensures even force distribution and reduced vibration.

This design delivers consistent accuracy during long production cycles and is ideal for fabrication units where smooth operation is essential.

Over Crank Power Shearing Machine

The Over Crank Power Shearing Machine is built for higher cutting force and faster stroke speed. The crank mechanism above the table provides greater mechanical advantage for heavy-duty production.

These machines are widely used in heavy fabrication units and industrial manufacturing plants where productivity is critical.
