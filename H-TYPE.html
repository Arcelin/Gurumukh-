H-Type Power Press

The H-Type Power Press is a heavy-duty mechanical press engineered for high-load, high-precision and continuous production operations. Its closed-frame “H” structure provides maximum rigidity, superior load distribution and minimal deflection.

The robust frame construction ensures excellent dimensional stability and long tool life, even under heavy tonnage operations. The powerful mechanical drive system delivers uniform force throughout the stroke, enabling accurate and repeatable results.

H-Type Power Presses are widely used in automotive component manufacturing, heavy fabrication, structural parts production and large-scale industrial applications.
