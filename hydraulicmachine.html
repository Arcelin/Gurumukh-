Hydraulic Shearing Machine

The Hydraulic Shearing Machine is designed for high-precision, smooth and consistent sheet cutting using a hydraulic power system. This machine offers uniform cutting force across the entire blade length, resulting in clean, burr-free cuts with minimal material distortion.

Its hydraulic operation ensures controlled stroke movement, reduced vibration and excellent repeatability, even during continuous production. Built with a rigid fabricated steel frame, the machine provides superior stability and long service life.

Hydraulic Shearing Machines are widely used in sheet metal fabrication units, steel furniture manufacturing, electrical panels, enclosures, HVAC ducting and industrial production facilities, where accuracy and surface finish are critical.

Designed for efficiency, safety and durability, the Hydraulic Shearing Machine from Gurumukh Machine Industries ensures dependable performance supported by strong after-sales service.

Hydraulic Bending Machine

The Hydraulic Bending Machine is engineered for accurate and uniform bending of sheet metal using hydraulic force. Designed to handle a wide range of bending applications, this machine provides precise angle control, consistent bending pressure and smooth operation.

The robust frame construction ensures minimal deflection during bending, maintaining dimensional accuracy across repeated operations. The hydraulic system allows smooth ram movement, enabling controlled bending of MS, SS and aluminium sheets.

Hydraulic Bending Machines are commonly used in sheet metal fabrication, enclosure manufacturing, furniture production, ducting fabrication and structural component manufacturing where precision and repeatability are essential.

Built for reliability and operational safety, the Hydraulic Bending Machine from Gurumukh Machine Industries is a dependable solution for workshops and industries seeking accurate bending with long-term performance.
