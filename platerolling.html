Plate Rolling Machine

Precision-built machines designed for accurate and uniform rolling of metal sheets into cylindrical, conical and curved shapes.

The Plate Rolling Machine is designed for accurate and uniform rolling of metal sheets into cylindrical, conical and curved shapes. Built for durability and precision, this machine is widely used in fabrication and manufacturing units where consistent rolling accuracy and smooth operation are required.

Constructed with a rigid fabricated steel frame, the machine provides excellent structural strength and stability during rolling operations. Precision-machined rollers ensure smooth material movement and uniform pressure distribution, resulting in accurate rolling with minimal surface marking. The robust mechanical system allows efficient handling of MS, SS and aluminium sheets across various thicknesses.

Plate Rolling Machines are commonly used in tank fabrication, pressure vessel manufacturing, chimney and duct fabrication, structural fabrication and general industrial production, where repeatability and dimensional accuracy are essential. The machine design allows easy material feeding, controlled rolling and reliable output, making it suitable for both batch production and custom fabrication work.

Manufactured with a focus on mechanical reliability, ease of operation and long service life, the Plate Rolling Machine from Gurumukh Machine Industries is a dependable solution for workshops and industries seeking precision rolling equipment.

Size & Specifications

Working Lengths: Available in multiple sizes to suit different fabrication requirements

Rolling Capacity: Suitable for Mild Steel (MS), Stainless Steel (SS) and Aluminium sheets within recommended thickness ranges

Roller Construction: Precision-machined hardened steel rollers

Operation: Manual, mechanical or power-operated models available

Frame Construction: Heavy-duty fabricated steel body for stable operation

Applications: Cylindrical rolling, cone rolling and curved sheet forming
