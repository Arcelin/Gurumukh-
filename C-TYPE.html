C-Type Power Press

The C-Type Power Press is a versatile mechanical press designed for precision punching, bending, blanking and forming operations. Its open “C” frame structure provides easy access from three sides, enabling quick die setting and efficient material handling.

Built with a rigid fabricated steel frame, the C-Type press ensures stable operation, smooth stroke movement and consistent accuracy during repetitive production cycles. The well-balanced mechanical drive system minimizes vibration and wear.

C-Type Power Presses are widely used in sheet metal fabrication, electrical components manufacturing, automotive parts, hardware production and general job-work applications.
