Under Crank Power Shearing Machine

Under Crank Power Shearing Machines are designed for stable, vibration-free and precise cutting operations. The crank mechanism is positioned below the cutting table to ensure uniform force distribution and smooth shearing action.

These machines are widely used in sheet metal fabrication units, automotive workshops, electrical panel manufacturing and general engineering industries where accuracy, durability and long production cycles are essential.
