Hydraulic Shearing Machine

The Hydraulic Shearing Machine is designed for high-precision, smooth and consistent sheet cutting using an advanced hydraulic power system. It delivers uniform cutting force across the entire blade length, resulting in clean, burr-free cuts with minimal material distortion.

The hydraulic operation ensures controlled stroke movement, reduced vibration and excellent repeatability even during continuous production. The rigid fabricated steel frame provides superior stability and long service life.

Hydraulic Shearing Machines are widely used in sheet metal fabrication, steel furniture manufacturing, electrical panels, enclosures, HVAC ducting and industrial production facilities where accuracy is critical.

Designed for efficiency, safety and durability, the Hydraulic Shearing Machine ensures dependable performance backed by strong after-sales service and technical support.
